About the project

Why I built this

As an Ed.D. student, I needed one place to organize research articles, chapter notes, deadlines, coursework, and writing goals. Traditional tools helped with pieces of the process, but I wanted a workspace designed around the dissertation journey itself.

My Dissertation Dashboard is a cloud-based application designed to support the full Dissertation in Practice workflow: planning, reading, writing, revising, and staying aware of what is coming next.

Features

Designed for dissertation work

📚

Research Library

Store articles, citation details, notes, quotes, and source summaries in one place.

✍️

Chapter Workspaces

Organize the five dissertation chapters with goals, notes, progress, and related research.

📅

Master Schedule

Track coursework, meetings, dissertation milestones, writing blocks, and deadlines.

✅

Task Manager

Prioritize dissertation and coursework tasks by category, deadline, and status.

☁️

Cloud Sync

Save securely with Firebase so the workspace can be accessed from different devices.

📱

Responsive Design

Use the dashboard on desktop, tablet, or phone with a clean, readable interface.

About me

Built as part of my Ed.D. journey

I am an Ed.D. student interested in educational leadership, practical problem-solving, and using technology to make complex academic work more manageable. My Dissertation Dashboard began as a personal system to support my own Dissertation in Practice and has grown into a custom application for organizing doctoral work.
